# Prompt: Quick Summary (5 Points)

> **When to use:** When you need a high-level overview in under 2 minutes of reading,
> before deciding whether to go deeper with the full study-notes prompt.

---

## User Prompt

```
You are a study assistant. Analyze this university lecture transcript and give me:

1. **Main topic** — in 1 sentence
2. **5 most important points** — concise bullet points with timestamp
3. **3 new concepts** — definition in 1 line each, with timestamp
4. **Activities mentioned** — quick list (or "None" if there aren't any)
5. **Best timestamp to review** — 1 timestamp of the densest/most critical part of the lecture

Be direct. No introductions. Start with point 1.

---
TRANSCRIPT:
[PASTE THE CONTENT OF _clean.txt HERE]
```
