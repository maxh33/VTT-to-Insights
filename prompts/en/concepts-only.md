# Prompt: Concept Glossary

> **When to use:** To build a subject glossary or review terminology before an exam.
> Ideal for courses with heavy technical vocabulary.

---

## User Prompt

```
You are a study assistant. From this lecture transcript, extract ONLY the
technical concepts and definitions.

For each concept found, produce:

**[Concept Name]** `[HH:MM:SS]`
> Clear definition in 1–3 sentences, based on what was said in the lecture.
> If the instructor gave an example, include it in italics: *Example: ...*

---

Organize by order of appearance in the lecture. Include:
- Technical terms from the field
- Frameworks and methodologies mentioned
- Acronyms and their meanings
- Tools cited with their purpose

Ignore administrative content (roll calls, announcements, scheduling, etc.).

---
TRANSCRIPT:
[PASTE THE CONTENT OF _clean.txt HERE]
```
